Business Continuity

Klass Chauffeur is Now Trouv Chauffeurs

Same legal entity. Same team. Same service standards. Registered name change at Companies House.

What Happened

A Name Change, Not a New Company

Klass Chauffeur Ltd was a premium chauffeur service operating across London and the United Kingdom, building a strong record of professional, discreet executive travel for corporate clients, hotels, travel advisors and private individuals.

The company has not closed, been sold, or replaced. Klass Chauffeur Ltd was legally renamed to Trouv Chauffeurs Limited through a registered company name change at Companies House. The company registration number remains the same. The directors, operational team and service standards are unchanged.

If you are a former Klass Chauffeur client, you are dealing with exactly the same organisation under our updated trading name.

The Transition

What Changed and What Did Not

What Changed

The trading name. Klass Chauffeur Ltd is now Trouv Chauffeurs Limited. The website address is trouvchauffeurs.co.uk. The contact email is info@trouvchauffeurs.co.uk. The base of operations has moved to Borehamwood, Hertfordshire.

What Did Not Change

The company registration number. The directors and ownership. The chauffeur team. The fleet of Mercedes-Benz S-Class, V-Class and Range Rover Autobiography vehicles. The service standards, pricing structure and approach to every journey.

Legal Confirmation

The name change from Klass Chauffeur Ltd to Trouv Chauffeurs Limited is registered at Companies House and can be verified by searching our company registration number on the Companies House public register.
