London Gatwick

London's Second-Largest International Airport

London Gatwick Airport is the UK's second busiest airport, located approximately 28 miles south of central London near Crawley in West Sussex. The airport serves over 45 million passengers annually and operates flights to more than 200 destinations across Europe, North America, the Caribbean, Asia and beyond.

Gatwick is divided into two terminals: the South Terminal and the North Terminal, connected by a rapid transit shuttle service that runs 24 hours a day. The South Terminal is the larger of the two, hosting easyJet, British Airways and a number of other short and medium-haul carriers. The North Terminal is home to several leading airlines including Norwegian, TUI and a range of European carriers.

Trouv provides seamless chauffeur transfers to and from both Gatwick terminals. Your chauffeur will monitor your flight in real time and meet you directly in the arrivals hall, holding a discreet name board. We allow generous complimentary waiting time on every pickup, giving you ample time to collect luggage and clear customs without any pressure.

Whether you are heading into central London, to a country estate in Surrey or anywhere across the South East, our Gatwick transfer service offers a calm, refined alternative to taxis and public transport. Our Mercedes-Benz fleet — including the S-Class, V-Class and Range Rover Autobiography — ensures every journey is comfortable and in complete keeping with the standard you expect.
