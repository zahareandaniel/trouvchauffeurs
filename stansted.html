London Stansted

Essex's Major Aviation Hub

London Stansted Airport is located approximately 35 miles north-east of central London in Essex, close to the town of Bishop's Stortford. It is the third busiest airport in the United Kingdom, serving over 28 million passengers annually and operating flights to more than 180 destinations across Europe, North Africa and the Middle East.

Stansted is served primarily by low-cost carriers, with Ryanair operating its largest base here, alongside Wizz Air, easyJet and a range of European and charter airlines. The airport operates from a single, purpose-built terminal designed by renowned architect Sir Norman Foster, which opened in 1991 and remains one of the most architecturally distinctive airport buildings in Europe. Its cathedral-like roof structure and efficient layout make it straightforward to navigate on arrival.

Trouv offers executive chauffeur transfers to and from Stansted with full flight monitoring and meet-and-greet service. Given the airport's distance from central London and the relative inconvenience of rail and coach alternatives, a private chauffeur service is one of the most practical and comfortable options available. Your chauffeur will be waiting in the arrivals hall with a name board, ready to assist with luggage and guide you to your vehicle.

Whether you are heading into London, to the home counties of Hertfordshire, Essex or beyond, Trouv will ensure your onward journey from Stansted is smooth, punctual and in complete comfort.
