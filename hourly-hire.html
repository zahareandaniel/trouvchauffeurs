Flexible Chauffeur

Your Chauffeur, Your Schedule

Some journeys cannot be defined by a single destination. When your day demands flexibility multiple meetings across the city, a morning of appointments followed by a private lunch, or an evening that moves between venues Trouv's hourly hire service places a dedicated chauffeur entirely at your disposal.

From the moment your chauffeur arrives, the clock is yours. There are no rigid routes, no fixed endpoints and no compromise on standard. Whether you require three hours or a full day, your chauffeur remains present, prepared and professional throughout managing routes in real time, anticipating your next requirement and ensuring every transition between commitments is seamless.

Trouv's hourly hire is the preferred choice for corporate executives managing demanding schedules, private clients with social engagements across London, and international visitors who require a trusted driver for the duration of their stay. It is also the natural solution for roadshows, investor days and board-level meetings where punctuality and discretion are non-negotiable.
