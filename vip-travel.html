VIP Service

Exceptional Travel for Exceptional Clients

For those who require more than a chauffeur who expect a travel experience that anticipates every need, eliminates every friction and operates with absolute discretion Trouv's VIP service represents the highest standard of private ground transport in London.

VIP travel with Trouv begins before the journey. Your dedicated account manager will coordinate every aspect of your transfer in advance: preferred vehicle, preferred route, onboard refreshments, specific requirements and any particular considerations relevant to your schedule or profile. Nothing is left to chance, and nothing is communicated that should not be.

Our VIP clients include executives travelling between private aviation terminals and city headquarters, high-net-worth individuals requiring ongoing personal chauffeur arrangements, visiting dignitaries, and private clients who value consistency, reliability and the confidence that comes from working with a team that knows their requirements before they are asked.

For clients where confidentiality is paramount, Trouv is able to operate under a non-disclosure agreement. Our chauffeurs are trained to the highest professional standard present, attentive and entirely discreet. There is no unnecessary conversation, no use of devices during transfers, and no discussion of client details outside of the service relationship.

Whether you require a single exceptional journey or an ongoing private chauffeur arrangement across weeks or months, Trouv's VIP service is built around you not around a schedule, a price list or a standard package.
