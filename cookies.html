1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Trouv Chauffeurs Limited (“we”, “us”, “Trouv”) approaches cookies and similar technologies on trouvchauffeurs.co.uk. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

We do not use this website to run analytics, advertising or social-media tracking. We do not place cookies or use localStorage to profile browsing behaviour or build marketing lists.

Not legal advice. This policy describes our practices; it does not constitute legal advice for your situation.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a site or service may store on your device. “Similar technologies” can include, for example, certain headers or storage used by hosting platforms for security or delivery.

3. What We Use (and Do Not Use)

We do not use: Google Analytics (or similar), advertising pixels, A/B testing trackers, or third-party embeds whose purpose is to track you across the web.

Hosting: Our website is hosted on infrastructure that may set strictly necessary cookies or use similar mechanisms for security, reliability and performance (for example to resist abuse or balance load). We do not control every technical name or duration of those tokens; they are not used by Trouv for marketing or analytics.

Fonts: We use system fonts loaded from your device only. We do not load fonts from third-party providers for this website.

Contact form: If you choose to send an enquiry, the details you type are processed to respond to you, as described in the Privacy Policy. That is not automatic tracking; it happens only when you submit the form.

4. Legal Bases

Where UK GDPR applies, strictly necessary technologies used to deliver and secure the website are typically justified on grounds of strict necessity and/or legitimate interests, balanced against your rights. We do not rely on consent for analytics or advertising cookies because we do not deploy them on this site.

5. Data Controller

Trouv Chauffeurs Limited is the data controller for personal data processed in connection with this website. Contact: info@trouvchauffeurs.co.uk. Registered office: 44 Linton Avenue, Borehamwood, WD6 4QZ, United Kingdom.

6. Browser Controls

You can block or delete cookies and clear site data using your browser settings. For general information about cookies, see the ICO. If you need help, contact info@trouvchauffeurs.co.uk.

7. Changes

If our practices change (for example if we ever add analytics), we will update this policy and the “Last updated” date above.
