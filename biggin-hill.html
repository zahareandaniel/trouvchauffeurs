Biggin Hill: History, Heritage and High-End Aviation

London Biggin Hill Airport carries one of the most distinguished histories of any airfield in the United Kingdom. Made famous by its pivotal role in the Battle of Britain during the Second World War, the airfield has since been transformed into one of London's most important private aviation hubs, now operating exclusively as a business and general aviation airport.

Situated in the London Borough of Bromley in the North Downs, approximately 14 miles south-east of central London, Biggin Hill offers a compelling combination of proximity to the capital and the privacy associated with dedicated business aviation facilities. The airport is home to Inflite Jet Centre, a world-class fixed-base operator that provides a full suite of handling, concierge and passenger services for private jet arrivals and departures.

The airport handles a wide variety of private aircraft, from turboprops and light jets through to large-cabin long-range jets serving destinations across Europe, the Middle East and beyond. For passengers seeking to avoid the delays and exposure of commercial airports, Biggin Hill provides a genuinely seamless alternative — with dedicated security, immigration and customs handled discreetly on site for international arrivals.

Trouv provides executive chauffeur transfers to and from Biggin Hill for private aviation passengers who expect the highest level of service on the ground. Your chauffeur will be positioned at the terminal, ready to greet you upon arrival and transport you in immaculate comfort to any destination across London or the surrounding counties. The entire experience is tailored around your schedule, your privacy and your preferences.
