RAF Northolt: London's Most Exclusive Airfield

RAF Northolt is a Royal Air Force station located in the London Borough of Hillingdon, approximately 13 miles west of central London and just a short distance from Heathrow Airport. It is the closest airfield to central London with a full-length runway, and serves as the primary destination for VIP government, royal, diplomatic and high-net-worth private flights into the capital.

Unlike commercial airports or even dedicated private aviation terminals, RAF Northolt operates under strict access controls managed by the Ministry of Defence. The airfield hosts the 32nd (The Royal) Squadron, which provides air transport for members of the Royal Family and senior government ministers, as well as handling a carefully vetted selection of private and charter aircraft for authorised passengers.

Passengers arriving at RAF Northolt benefit from an exceptionally fast and private transit experience. With dedicated handling, on-site immigration and a secure environment, the transfer from aircraft to ground transport can be completed in minutes. For heads of state, government officials, senior executives and high-profile individuals, this level of speed and security is often the principal reason for choosing Northolt over other London airports.

Trouv has extensive experience providing executive chauffeur services to passengers transiting through RAF Northolt. Our chauffeurs understand the protocols associated with this unique facility and are thoroughly prepared to meet the timing, security and discretion requirements involved. Your vehicle will be positioned at the designated handover point, and your journey will proceed without delay or unnecessary attention. Every element of your transfer is handled with the same professionalism that RAF Northolt itself demands.
