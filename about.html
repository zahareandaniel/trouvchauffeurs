About Trouv Chauffeurs: Based in Borehamwood, Formerly Klass Chauffeur

A Borehamwood-based chauffeur company, formerly trading as Klass Chauffeur. Built on discretion, professionalism and consistency.

Who We Are

Premium Chauffeur Service, Based in Borehamwood

Trouv Chauffeurs is a premium chauffeur service based in Borehamwood, Hertfordshire, providing executive travel for corporate clients, fashion and luxury brands, hotels, travel advisors and private individuals across the United Kingdom.

We operate with a clear focus: to deliver reliable, discreet and carefully handled journeys for clients who expect professionalism as standard, not as an exception.

Our approach is understated. We don't believe in loud promises or exaggerated claims. We believe in consistently doing the work well, journey after journey, client after client.

Our History

Formerly Trading as Klass Chauffeur

Trouv Chauffeurs Limited is the legal continuation of Klass Chauffeur Ltd, a premium chauffeur company that built a strong reputation for professional, discreet executive travel across London and the United Kingdom.

Following a registered change of company name at Companies House, the business now trades as Trouv Chauffeurs Limited. The company registration number, operational team and service standards remain unchanged. This is a rebrand, not a new company.

Clients who previously travelled with Klass Chauffeur are travelling with the same organisation, the same team and the same vehicles, under our updated trading name. Our base has moved to Borehamwood, Hertfordshire, from which we continue to serve London, all major airports and the wider United Kingdom.

How We Work

What Defines Us

Discretion

We understand that many of our clients value privacy above all. Our chauffeurs are trained to be professional, courteous and discreet. No visible branding on any vehicle.

Professionalism

From the first enquiry to the final drop-off, every interaction is handled with care. Our chauffeurs present in business attire, and every vehicle is impeccably maintained.

Consistency

We don't aim to impress once. We aim to deliver the same standard of service on every journey, whether it's a five-minute transfer or a full-day corporate hire.
