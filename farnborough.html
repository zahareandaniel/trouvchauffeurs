Farnborough: The Preferred Gateway for Private Jet Passengers

Farnborough Airport is widely regarded as one of the finest private aviation airports in Europe. Located in Hampshire approximately 35 miles south-west of central London, it operates exclusively as a business aviation terminal, meaning every passenger and every aircraft on site is private. There are no commercial queues, no public terminals and no compromise on the level of service or privacy afforded to each passenger.

The airport is home to TAG Aviation, one of the world's leading private aviation companies, and offers a dedicated fixed-base operator (FBO) facility with private lounges, bespoke passenger handling and an on-site customs and immigration team. Passengers arriving on international private jets can clear border control on site in a matter of minutes, in an entirely private setting — a level of efficiency and discretion simply not available at commercial airports.

Farnborough is also the venue for the world-famous Farnborough International Airshow, held biennially and attracting aerospace industry leaders from across the globe. During airshow years, demand for executive ground transport to and from the airport surges considerably, and Trouv is well-positioned to provide seamless transfers for corporate delegations and VIP guests.

Trouv's chauffeur service to Farnborough is perfectly aligned with the expectations of private jet passengers. Your vehicle will be airside or at the FBO steps by arrangement, ready to depart the moment you are ready. Every journey is conducted with absolute discretion, in an immaculate Mercedes-Benz vehicle, by a professionally presented chauffeur who understands the standards of private aviation travel.
