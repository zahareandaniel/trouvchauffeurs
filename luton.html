Luton: A Growing Gateway for London

London Luton Airport is situated approximately 32 miles north of central London in Bedfordshire, close to the M1 motorway. Having undergone extensive redevelopment in recent years, Luton now serves over 18 million passengers annually and operates flights to more than 100 destinations across Europe and beyond.

The airport is a key base for Wizz Air and Ryanair, with additional services operated by easyJet and a range of holiday and charter carriers. Luton has seen significant investment in its terminal facilities, car parks and drop-off zones as passenger numbers have grown steadily, making it one of the most important regional airports serving the greater London area.

Travelling to and from Luton by private chauffeur is often the most convenient option, particularly for passengers carrying luggage or travelling in groups. While train and coach links to central London exist, they involve transfers and can be unpredictable during busy periods. A Trouv chauffeur transfer eliminates those complications entirely — your driver will be waiting on arrival, assist with all luggage, and take the most efficient route to your destination.

Trouv monitors all flights in real time for every Luton pickup, adjusting for delays or early arrivals automatically. We provide a discreet, professional meet-and-greet service inside the terminal, and our vehicles are always clean, immaculate and fully equipped to ensure you travel in comfort from the moment you step outside the terminal.
